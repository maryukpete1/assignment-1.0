Biography

My name is Ukpete Mary Aghoghoibede. I am currently studying back end engineering in Altschool while also chasing my Bachelors degree in Metallurgical and materials engineering in the Univeristy of Lagos. I hail from Okpara Inland, Delta state. I was born and bred in Lagos. I am passionate about giving back to the society and volunteering. I am currently the welfare secretary of the National Association of Metallurgical and materials Engineering Students, Univeristy of Lagos chapter. I am also the public relations officer and content team lead for the Associationof Professional Women Engineers Of Nigeria, University of Lagos chapter. I enjoy reading a lot. My favorite genre is African literature and romance. I am a Christian and I also hope to change the world some day.
